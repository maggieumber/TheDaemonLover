The Rock

Giving up life
to join a fomer lover in hell

History

1968: Story History

Shirley Jackson's The Rock was published posthumously in Come Along With Me in 1968.

2018: Comic Production

Thumbnail storyboards were completed in January 2019. Comic pages were begun in February 2019. The completed comic will be 72 pages.

Process

Some pages were penciled ahead of time, some were not. All pages were painted in gouache on cold press watercolor paper.
