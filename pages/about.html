The Daemon Lover
James Harris

History

1949: Story History

Shirley Jackson's The Witch was published in The Lottery: or, The Adventures of James Harris in 1949. The title and James Harris as "The Witch" figure are present in the comic version. There are no other similarities.

2019-2020: Comic Production

Thumbnail storyboards were completed in January 2019. Comic pages were begun in February 2019. The completed comic will be 72 pages.
