The Intoxicated

An encounter with James Harris
at a drunken party

History

Early 1940s: Story History

Shirley Jackson wrote her short story The Intoixcated in the early 1940s. It was published in her short story collection The Lottery: or, The Adventures of James Harris in 1949. The title, drunken party and James Harris are the only parts that end up in the comic.

2018: Comics Production

The storyboards were drawn in a day in July 2018. The trace monotypes were printed from the end of July to early August 2018.

2020: Publication History

This comic appears in Now: The New Comics Anthology #8, edited by Eric Reynolds, published by Fantagraphics.

Process

These trace monotypes were printed with relief ink on newsprint using pencil, crayon, marker and finger pressure.
