History

1956 or earlier: Story History

On Looking Into the Eyes of a Demon Lover was composed by Sylvia Plath. It was published posthumously in The Collected Poems. Some of the imagery informed this comic.

Process

2018: Comic Production

The storyboards were made one day in September 2018. The 12 page comic was drawn between September and November 2018.

This comic was drawn with water-soluble crayon, watercolor pencil and water brush on polypropylene watercolor paper.
