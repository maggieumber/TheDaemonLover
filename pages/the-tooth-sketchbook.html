The Tooth

a trip to the dentist or
A modern variation on the Daemon Lover ballad

History

1947-1949: Story History

Shirley Jackson wrote her short story The Tooth in 1947. It was published in her short story collection The Lottery: or, The Adventures of James Harris in 1949.

2016: Comic Production

The 24 page comic was drawn from mid-May to early June 2016.

2016: Publication History

It appeared in The Shirley Jackson Project, edited by Roby Kirby and published by Ninth Art Press, in 2016.
